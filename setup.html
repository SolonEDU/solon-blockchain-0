Metamask Setup

Steps to set up your Metamask account

  1. Install the Metamask extention to your Google Chrome, Firefox, or Opera browser from the website https://metamask.io/
  2. Create a password to access your account.
  3. Accept the terms that follow.
  4. Eventually you will get to a page with a seed phrase. This step is very important! Your seed phrase is a set of 12 random words in a specific order that is specific to your account. Because of this, write your seed phrase down somewhere where you'll remember it. DON'T share your seed phrase with anyone else! In the next screen, you will be asked to enter your seed phrase.
  5. Enter your seed phrase in the correct order.
  6. Congratulations, you have successfully set up a Metamask account!
